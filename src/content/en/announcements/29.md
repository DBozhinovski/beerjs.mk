<img src="/img/29.png" />

## Presentations

1. **Effect - You Can't Be Serious** - Nikola Demerdjiev
   
> I am serious. And don't call me TypeError: Cannot read properties of undefined

   TypeScript catches quite a few bugs before they reach runtime, but not those that will keep you awake at 3 AM. In this presentation, we will explore what Effect is—the library that includes type-safe errors, DI, and composable async in TypeScript.

2. **Don't leave randomness to chance** - Vekoslav Stefanovski
   
   Deep dive into the world of random—this presentation will explore the technical details and theory behind the concept of randomness in JavaScript.
