## Presentations

1. **What Color is Your Component?** - Darko Bozhinovski

   A decade ago, Bob Nystrom's "What Color is Your Function?" changed how we think about async. But in modern frameworks, color isn't just sync vs async anymore - it's reactivity, server vs client, data fetching, and lifecycle, all bleeding into each other. We'll tour the color problem as it exists in JS frameworks today, look at how Solid 2 tries to eliminate it with colorless logic and non-blocking UI, and unpack why understanding code color will change how you think about every framework you use.

2. **Living on the edge - serverless edge JS with Cloudflare Workers** - Ilija Boshkov

   Forget regions and cold starts - Cloudflare Workers runs your JavaScript at the edge, milliseconds away from every user on the planet. We'll explore what edge computing actually means, how Workers differs from traditional serverless, and how to start shipping JS on the edge easily.
